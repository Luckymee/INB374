Product & Customer

The product & customer service is a RESTful based web service that interacts with the product and customer database tables. The service endpoint can be found here, customer endpoint here and product endpoint here.

Functionality

Customer Endpoint

Allows for the sales team to either retrieve existing customer details or add new customers.

Product Endpoint

Encapsulates a list of all products currently available and their attributes for use by the sales team.
