Warehouse

The warehouse service is a WSDL based web service that allows the sales team to interact with the warehouse. The service can be found here, its associated WSDL file here.

Functionality

getWaitTime

Returns the delivery time of stock to the store.

getSupplierWaitTime

Returns the delivery time of stock from the supplier to the warehouse.

orderStock

Iterates through the warehouse and re stocks any item with a stock level below three.
